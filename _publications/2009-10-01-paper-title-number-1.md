This work used molecular dynamics to investigate histidine crystallization in aqueous-ethanol systems and identified a three-step mechanism involving competitive binding and dewetting during crystal formation.

The paper connects molecular-scale structure and solvent effects to experimentally relevant crystallization behavior.
