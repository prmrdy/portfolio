This research theme focuses on understanding how crystallization emerges from molecular interactions in solution. I use molecular dynamics and mechanism-based analysis to study solvation, desolvation, and crystal formation pathways in small-molecule systems under experimentally relevant conditions.

The broader goal is to connect molecular insight with crystallization process design, especially in systems where competing interactions and intermediate states control nucleation and growth behavior.
