My research lies at the intersection of crystallization science, molecular modeling, and materials synthesis. This page highlights a few of the research directions that define my work.

My work combines theory, computation, and experiment, with tools that include molecular dynamics, microkinetic modeling, population balance models, DFT-supported analysis, in-situ scattering, spectroscopy, wet milling, and materials synthesis.
